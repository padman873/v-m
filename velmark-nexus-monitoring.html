Signal Coverage

What Velmark Nexus Monitors

Six core signal categories evaluated continuously across every VELMARK Digital project.

Technical SEO Health

Site crawlability, indexation status, Core Web Vitals, page speed, mobile usability, canonical signals, internal linking structure, and sitemap integrity — the foundations of search engine discoverability.

  • Crawl error detection
  • Core Web Vitals tracking
  • Mobile usability signals

Answer Engine Readiness (AEO)

How clearly your content answers specific questions — evaluated against the standards that AI assistants and answer engines use to extract, verify, and surface business information to users.

  • FAQ content quality scoring
  • Question-answer alignment
  • Featured snippet eligibility

Generative Engine Signals (GEO)

Visibility in generative AI responses from ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Claude — tracking how well your business is understood and recommended by systems that synthesise answers rather than list links.

Entity & Brand Clarity

How clearly your organization, its founder, location, services, and authority signals are communicated to both users and machines — including structured data validation and entity disambiguation.

Content Clarity & Depth

Content quality evaluation across pages — checking topical coverage, semantic relevance, reading clarity, content gaps relative to competitor pages, and freshness signals that influence ongoing ranking.

Trust & Authority Signals

E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) signals — including founder identity, business registration transparency, review signals, and backlink quality indicators.

The Three Visibility Pillars

SEO · AEO · GEO — Explained

Modern search visibility spans three distinct layers. Velmark Nexus monitors all three, because ignoring any one of them is leaving discoverability on the table.

Pillar 01

SEO

Search Engine Optimisation

The foundation. Traditional SEO ensures your website appears in Google and Bing results for relevant searches. It covers technical performance, keyword targeting, backlinks, and content quality.

What Nexus tracks

  • Page indexation and crawl status
  • Core Web Vitals (LCP, CLS, FID)
  • Keyword ranking signals
  • Internal link health
  • Backlink profile quality
2026 Priority

Pillar 02

AEO

Answer Engine Optimisation

The rising layer. AI assistants, voice search, and featured snippets all need content that directly and clearly answers specific questions. AEO structures your information so machines can extract and trust it.

What Nexus tracks

  • FAQ content completeness
  • Question-answer structure quality
  • Featured snippet eligibility
  • Schema FAQPage validation
  • Voice search readiness

Pillar 03

GEO

Generative Engine Optimisation

The frontier. Generative AI systems like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity synthesise answers from multiple sources. GEO ensures your business is understood, trusted, and recommended in these environments.

What Nexus tracks

  • Entity clarity for AI systems
  • Service and location signal strength
  • Citation and source potential
  • AI recommendation readiness
  • Generative summary clarity

What is Velmark Nexus?

Velmark Nexus is the internal monitoring and intelligence framework used by VELMARK Digital to continuously evaluate a website's crawlability, content clarity, answer-readiness, entity strength, and AI search visibility. It covers traditional SEO signals, AEO readiness for answer engines, and GEO signals for generative AI systems — providing a comprehensive, ongoing picture of how well a business is discovered and understood across all major digital search environments.

What is the difference between SEO, AEO, and GEO?

SEO (Search Engine Optimisation) targets traditional Google and Bing rankings through technical performance, keyword signals, and content quality. AEO (Answer Engine Optimisation) structures content so AI-powered answer engines can extract and surface precise answers to user questions. GEO (Generative Engine Optimisation) focuses on making a business understandable and recommendable to generative AI systems like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity, which synthesise answers rather than list links.

What is Agentic SEO?

Agentic SEO is a continuous, AI-assisted optimisation workflow that monitors search visibility signals, identifies content gaps, and implements improvements systematically — rather than relying on periodic manual audits. The "agentic" element refers to the use of automated monitoring and AI-assisted analysis to keep optimisation ongoing and responsive to search environment changes, which is a core principle of how Velmark Nexus operates.

Why do Malaysian businesses need GEO in 2026?

In 2026, a growing share of commercial search queries are being answered by generative AI systems before a user ever clicks a link. If a business is not structured, described, and trusted clearly enough for AI systems to understand and recommend it, it is effectively invisible in this emerging discovery layer. Malaysian businesses that invest in GEO now build a compounding advantage over competitors who are still optimising only for traditional blue-link results.
