FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)?

AEO is the practice of structuring content so answer engines and AI systems can more easily understand, extract, and surface clear business information.

How does Agentic SEO work?

Agentic SEO uses automation and AI-assisted workflows to monitor content opportunities, technical clarity, and visibility signals in a more continuous way.

What is GEO in modern search?

GEO, or generative engine optimization, focuses on making your business more understandable to generative systems that synthesize answers rather than only listing blue links.

What does Velmark Nexus monitor?

Velmark Nexus is positioned to monitor crawlability, content clarity, answer-readiness, entity strength, and visibility opportunities across evolving search environments.

Why show founder and registration details on the website?

Clear identity signals help users trust the business and help search systems better understand the organization behind the website.
